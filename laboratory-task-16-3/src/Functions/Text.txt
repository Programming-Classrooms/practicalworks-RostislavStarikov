Faculty of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science 
of the Belarusian State University, founded on April 1, 1970.
The Faculty was opened in accordance with the order 
of the rector of the university, academician of the Academy 
of Sciences of the BSSR A.N. Sevchenko on April 1, 
1970 on the basis of three departments: computational mathematics, 
applied mathematics, differential equations.
